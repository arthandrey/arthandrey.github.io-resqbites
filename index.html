ABOUT US

At ResQBites, we're committed to reducing food waste while supporting local communities.

We are dedicated to fighting food waste and promoting sustainability through our innovative mobile app.

Our platform connects food businesses, customers, and nonprofit organizations to help reduce food waste while benefiting all parties involved. Food businesses, from cafés to restaurants, can use the app to offer unsold but still edible food to customers at discounted prices, helping them increase revenue while minimizing waste. Consumers can easily access affordable, ready-to-eat meals, and nonprofit organizations focused on food rescue and hunger relief can benefit donations and excess food distribution. By offering user profiles, seamless order processing, secure payment options, and delivery features, ResQBites supports businesses in reducing waste, boosting profits, and giving back to the community.

OUR SERVICES

FOOD BUSINESS PARTNERSHIP

We offer food businesses, from cafés to restaurants, an easy and efficient way to reduce waste and increase revenue. Our platform allows businesses to sell their unsold but still edible food to customers at discounted prices.

SEAMLESS ORDERING AND DELIVERY

Our user-friendly app makes it simple to browse available food options, place orders, and arrange for delivery. Whether you're ordering from a local café or receiving a food donation, our platform ensures a smooth transaction.

AFFORDABLE MEAL ACCESS FOR CUSTOMERS

As a consumer, ResQBites gives you access to delicious, ready-to-eat meals at affordable prices. Whether you're looking for a quick snack or a full meal, our app connects you with local food businesses offering surplus food.
